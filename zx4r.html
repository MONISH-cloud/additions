Kawasaki ZX-4R

The **Kawasaki ZX-4R** is a compact, high-performance motorcycle designed to offer thrilling experiences in both track and street riding. It features a 399cc inline-four engine that delivers impressive power for its size, along with advanced electronics to ensure precise control. The ZX-4R has a sharp, aerodynamic design, optimized handling, and premium suspension components, making it perfect for riders looking to experience the excitement of a smaller, nimble sportbike with the power of a bigger machine. Its aggressive styling and cutting-edge technology make it a standout choice for enthusiasts.





Specification Details
Engine Capacity 399 cc
Mileage 18+ kmpl
Transmission 6 Speed Manual
Kerb Weight 188 kg
Fuel Tank Capacity 15 litres
Seat Height 785 mm
