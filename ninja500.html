Kawasaki Ninja 500

The **Kawasaki Ninja 500** is a versatile middleweight sportbike, known for its smooth performance and approachable power. With a 498cc engine, it provides a great balance of handling and power delivery. It's suitable for both beginners and experienced riders who want a reliable and fun motorcycle for everyday use. The Ninja 500 is perfect for city commuting, as well as weekend trips on winding roads. Its light weight, agile handling, and sporty appearance make it a standout in its class.





Specification Details
Engine Capacity 498 cc
Mileage 20+ kmpl
Transmission 6 Speed Manual
Kerb Weight 192 kg
Fuel Tank Capacity 17 litres
Seat Height 790 mm
